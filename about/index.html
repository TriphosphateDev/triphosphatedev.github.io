* About Our Revision Policy

While I proudly offer unlimited revisions to ensure your satisfaction, this policy operates within reasonable limits and at my discretion:

  • Guaranteed Revisions: A specific number of revisions is guaranteed in our agreement to align with your project's scope.
  • Flexibility Beyond Scope: Additional revisions outside the agreement are often provided free of charge but are subject to my discretion based on the project's progress.
  • Clear Feedback: To keep the process efficient, I kindly ask for detailed and focused feedback to ensure timely and high-quality results.
