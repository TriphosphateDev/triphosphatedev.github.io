Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes, I offer a free consultation to discuss your project, goals, and how I can help. Reach out through the contact form, email, or Discord to schedule yours!

What services do you offer?

I provide professional audio mixing tailored for independent artists and bedroom producers. My services include vocal enhancement, track balancing, and custom sound sculpting to match your vision.

Who are your services best suited for?

I specialize in working with up-and-coming artists, bedroom producers, and vocalists who want to elevate their sound without breaking the bank.

What genres do you work with?

I'm experienced in mixing a wide range of genres, including pop, hip-hop, rock, electronic, and indie. If you're unsure, feel free to reach out to discuss your project.

How much do your services cost?

My pricing is affordable and designed with independent artists in mind. Contact me for a quote tailored to your specific needs.

How does the revision process work?

I'm flexible with revisions and will work closely with you to ensure the final mix meets your expectations. While revisions are nearly unlimited within reason, the goal is to refine your track while staying true to your creative vision.

How long does it take to complete a mix?

Most mixes are completed within 3-5 business days, depending on the complexity of the project. Faster turnaround times can be arranged if needed.

How should I prepare my tracks for mixing?

Ensure your tracks are exported as high-quality WAV files with no effects applied. Include clear labels for each file and a reference track, if possible, to communicate your vision.

What makes you different from other mix engineers?

I'm known for my flexibility, collaboration, and ability to channel an artist's unique vision. I combine professional-quality results with affordable pricing, designed for independent artists.

Can I hear examples of your work?

Yes! Check out my portfolio on SoundCloud or EngineEars to hear some of my past projects.

How can I contact you?

You can reach me through the Contact Form, email me at triphosphateLP@gmail.com, or send me a message on Discord.
